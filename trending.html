Games To Try

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game created by miHoYo. Player can explore large areas, fight powerful enemies, and find secrets of an unknown world.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, Android, Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 5, iOS
Genres- Action role-playing game, Nonlinear gameplay.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role-playing video game developed by CD Projekt Red, developer of The Witcher games series. It was released in September 2020. It also starred keanu reeves as one of the game character.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Google Stadia, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and Series S, Microsoft Windows.

Battlefield V

Battlefield V is a first-person shooter video game created by DICE and published by Electronic Arts. Battlefield V is part of the Battlefield game series. Player can experience greatest conflict as Battlefield goes back to its WW2.
Platforms- Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Rockstar Games developed and published Red Dead Redemption 2 in October 2018. Rockstar Games is also creator of Grand Theft Auto V. Red Dead Redemption 2 is a action-adventure and survival game. It has won many awards and was Nominee for Game Of The Year award in 2018.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Google Stadia, Microsoft Windows.

Hitman 2

Published by Warner Bros and created by IO Interactive Hitman 2 is a stealth video game. Player has to travel the globe, tracking and eliminating the targets in differents locations. Hitman 2 provide wide variety of exotic locations. And is part of the Hitman game series.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows, Google Stadia.

Beyond: Two Souls

Beyond: Two Souls is an interactive drama and action-adventure game. It was created by Quantic Dream and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The designer of the game is David Cage. It was one of the most popular game in 2013 with IMDb rating of 8.5. Starring Elliot Page, Willem Dafoe and Eric Winter it has won many awards.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, Microsoft Windows.

Forza Horizon 4

Forza Horizon 4 is a racing video game created by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios. It was released on 2 October 2018. It has won The Game Award for Best Sports/Racing Game and BAFTA Games Award for British Game. Best game for player who love speed.
Platforms- Xbox One, Microsoft Windows, Xbox Series X and Series S.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Created by FromSoftware and published by Activision Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure video game. It was released in March 2019. In game you are the “one-armed wolf” who take revenge on a samurai clan. It has won Game of the Year Award in 2019 and The Game Award for Best Action/Adventure.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Google Stadia, Microsoft Windows.

Detroit: Become Human

Detroit: Become Human is a adventure video game created by Quantic Dream and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released in April 2018. Director David Cage trys to puts the destiny of both mankind and androids in your hands, taking you to a near future where machines have become more intelligent. And your decision will decide fate of 3 androids. Starring Jesse Williams,Valorie Curry and Bryan Dechart it has won many awards.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Microsoft Windows.
Genres- Action-adventure game, Adventure.

Paladins

Paladins: Champions of the Realm is Free-to-Play objective-based team first-person shooter video game. It was developed and published by Hi Rez Studios in September 2016. Hi Rez is also makers of SMITE. Being Multiplayer video game you can enjoy with yours friends.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, Microsoft Windows, Xbox Series X and Series S.
Genres- Multiplayer online battle arena, Real-time strategy, Action role-playing game, Hero shooter.

Call of Duty: Black Ops III

Call of Duty: Black Ops III created by Treyarch and published by Activision. It is a first-person shooter video game. It is sequel to the 2012 Call of Duty: Black Ops II video game and 25th part in the Call of Duty video game series. Jack wall composed the game and it is released in August 2015.
Platforms- PlayStation 3/4, Xbox One, Xbox 360, Microsoft Windows, macOS.

Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is a free-to-play online multiplayer first-person shooter video game created by Bungie. It was released in 2017. To explore the mysteries of the solar system and experience responsive first-person shooter video game dive into the world of Destiny 2. It was composed by Michael Salvatori and Skye Lewin.
Platforms- PlayStation 4/5, Xbox One, Google Stadia, Xbox Series X and Series S, Microsoft Windows and Xbox cloud gaming.
Genres- Multiplayer online game, First-person shooter, Platform game, Fighting game, Adventure game and Action role-playing game.

Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons is an action role-playing game dungeon crawler video game created by Mojang Studios, Double Eleven and published by Xbox Game Studios. It is a part of the Minecraft video game series and was released in May 2020. Explore new environments and set off on exciting quests in this video game.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and Series S, Nintendo Switch, Microsoft Windows, Xbox Cloud Gaming.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Published by Ubisoft and created by Ubisoft Quebec Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action role-playing video game. It was released in October 2018. It is part of Assassin's Creed video game series and continuation of Assassin's Creed Origins video game of 2017.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, Google Stadia, Microsoft Windows.
Genres- Fighting game, Shooter Video Game, Action role-playing game, Nonlinear gameplay, Adventure.

Star Wars Battlefront II

Star Wars Battlefront II is an action shooter video game developed by DICE, Motive Studios and Criterion Software. It is a Single-player as well as Multiplayer video game released in November 2017. As it is based on the Star Wars franchise, the game has become popular.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows.
Genres- Third-person shooter, Adventure game.

Shadow of the Tomb Raider

Free-To-Play

Created by Eidos-Montréal and published by Square Enix's European subsidiary Shadow of the Tomb Raider is a action-adventure video game. It is continuation of Rise of the Tomb Raider game of 2015 and is part of Tomb Raider game series. It was released in September 2018 as a Single-player video game. Experience Lara Croft's adventure.
Platforms- PlayStation 5/4, Microsoft Windows, Xbox Series X and Series S.
