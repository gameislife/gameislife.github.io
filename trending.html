Games To Try

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an free-to-play role playing video game created by miHoYo. Player has to explore large areas, fight enemies with different magical powers, Use various type of weapons including swords and bows. After reaching Adventure Rank 16 you can play with your friends till then you have to play solo. To enjoy this video game fully, play it on PC with high quality graphics.
It come under categories of Action role-playing game and Nonlinear gameplay.
Platforms- PlayStation 4/5, PC(windows), iOS and Android devices.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role playing video game created by CD Projekt Red. CD Projekt Red is same which developed The Witcher video game series. It was released in September 2020. It also starred keanu reeves as one of the game character, which make it quite popular worldwide before its actual release. The game is based on a futuristic era with advance technology.
Suggestion- don't spend too much time in making your avatar because you cannot see yourself except in mirror as it is FFP game.
Platforms- PlayStation 4/5, Google Stadia, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S and PC(windows).

Battlefield V

Battlefield V is a first person shooter video game created by DICE and published by Electronic Arts. Battlefield V is part of the Battlefield game series. Players who like shooting game will definately love this video game as it goes back to WW2. Graphics of this game is very nice.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Rockstar Games created and published Red Dead Redemption 2. It was released in October 2018. Rockstar Games is also creator of Grand Theft Auto V and other very popular games. Red Dead Redemption 2 is a action-adventure and survival game. It has won many awards and was Nominee for Game Of The Year award in 2018.

Hitman 2

Published by Warner Bros and created by IO Interactive Hitman 2 is a stealth video game. Player has to travel the world, tracking and eliminating the targets in differents locations. Hitman 2 provide wide variety of exotic locations. And is part of the Hitman game series. Players can find different ways to eliminate particular target. You decide how you want to eliminate someone.

Beyond: Two Souls

Beyond: Two Souls is an interactive drama and action-adventure game. It was created by Quantic Dream and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The designer of the game is David Cage. It was one of the most popular game in 2013 with IMDb rating of 8.5. Starring Elliot Page, Willem Dafoe and Eric Winter it has won many awards. Player in this game has to make different choices resulting in different outcome.
Platforms- PlayStation 3/4 and PC(windows).

Forza Horizon 4

Forza Horizon 4 is a racing video game created by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios. It was released on 2 October 2018. It has won The Game Award for Best Sports/Racing Game and BAFTA Games Award for British Game. Best game for player who love speed. The game provides you with high quality graphics. And you can drive latest cars also.
Platforms- Xbox One, PC(windows) and Xbox Series X/S.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Created by FromSoftware and published by Activision Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure video game. It was released in March 2019. In game you play the role of 'one-armed wolf' who take revenge on a samurai clan. This game give great fighting experience like a ninja. Player can use different weapons too. It has won Game of the Year Award in 2019 and The Game Award for Best Action/Adventure.
Platforms- PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Google Stadia and PC(windows).

Detroit: Become Human

Detroit: Become Human is a adventure video game created by Quantic Dream and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released in April 2018. Director David Cage trys to puts the destiny of both mankind and androids in player hands, taking you to an advance time where robots(or Android) have become more intelligent. And your decision will decide fate of 3 androids. Game ends in many different ways depending on player choices. Starring Jesse Williams,Valorie Curry and Bryan Dechart it has won many awards.
This game come under categories of Action-adventure game and Adventure.
Platforms- PlayStation 4 and PC(windows).
